In Case of Emergency

Know exactly what to do in an emergency.

For serious injuries or medical emergencies
Dial 911
From any city phone dial 9-911
Also remember —
01
All Staff
Notify your supervisor of any injury immediately. No matter how minor the injury.
02
Supervisors
Supervisors must notify their safety professional whenever employees are transported to the emergency room.
